Join ChessPressHQ

Founded by Mr. Daksh Hande in 2025, ChessPressHQ is a leading platform delivering free chess news, updates, and strategies to enthusiasts worldwide. Our motto, "Empowering Every Move, Inspiring Every Mind," drives our mission to make chess accessible to all, no account required.

We’re looking for passionate individuals to join our team and help us grow our community of chess lovers. Whether you’re a writer, editor, or marketer, there’s a place for you at ChessPressHQ!

Our Work Culture

At ChessPressHQ, we value creativity, passion for chess, and a commitment to delivering high-quality content. Our team works remotely, fostering flexibility and collaboration across the globe. We believe in empowering our employees to make strategic moves in their careers, just like in a chess game.
