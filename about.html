About ChessPressHQ

Founded in 2025 by Mr. Daksh Hande, ChessPressHQ is a dedicated platform for chess enthusiasts worldwide. Our goal is to deliver free, high-quality chess news, updates, and strategies to players of all levels, from beginners to grandmasters.

Our motto, "Empowering Every Move, Inspiring Every Mind," reflects our commitment to fostering a deeper appreciation for chess through engaging content and accessible resources.

Our Mission

At ChessPressHQ, we aim to make chess news and insights freely available to everyone, no account required. We provide timely updates on tournaments, player profiles, and strategic tips to inspire and educate the global chess community.

Our Operations

We offer a wealth of free content, including:

  • News: Up-to-date coverage of chess tournaments and events.
  • Features: In-depth articles and player interviews.
  • Learning Resources: Tips and strategies to improve your game.

Our revenue comes from two streams:

  • Subscriptions: Our SuperGrok plan offers an ad-free experience and exclusive content like advanced strategy guides. Learn more.
  • Ads: Non-subscribers see non-intrusive banner ads, ensuring free access to all core content.

No user account is required, making ChessPressHQ accessible to everyone, anytime.

Our Founder

Mr. Daksh Hande founded ChessPressHQ in 2025 with a vision to unite chess lovers through a platform that celebrates the game's intellectual and strategic depth. His passion for chess drives our commitment to delivering quality content to a global audience.
